Q.6: What is async and defer ?

Async => The async  is an attribute. The script is downloaded in parallel(in the background) to parsing the page, and executed as soon as it is available and donâ€™t wait for anything.

for example : <script src="async.js" async></script>


Defer => The defer attribute is a boolean attribute. The script is downloaded in parallel(in the background) to parsing the page, and executed after the page has finished parsing. The defer attribute tells the browser not to wait for the script. Instead, the browser will continue to process the HTML, build DOM.

for example : <script src="defer.js" defer></script>
